The Birth of HELIX

Unity Two strands, one force
March 2026

Every great alliance has a defining moment — a point where it evolves into something greater than it was before. For Horizon, that moment came when we reached out to OITS, one of our closest allies.

The message was simple: join us. Not as guests. Not as a sub-alliance. As equals.

20 OITS members answered the call. They brought their strength, their experience, and their loyalty. We welcomed every single one with open arms.

"Two strong strands coming together — that's what a helix is."
— Alliance Leadership

To honor this merger, we changed our name. Horizon + OITS = HELIX. The tag became [HLIX], and a new chapter began.

We are no longer just Horizon. We are no longer just OITS. We are HELIX — intertwined, inseparable, and stronger than either strand alone. 🧬

The Betrayal of Powerstrokeking123

Betrayal The wound that never heals
January 2026

In the early days of Horizon, a player named powerstrokeking123 sought refuge among our ranks. We welcomed him. We shared our strategies. We trusted him.

He joined us with one purpose: to attack WOUT from the inside. We thought he was one of us. We were wrong.

After the attack, he vanished — slipping back into the shadows, leaving us to face the consequences. WOUT retaliated. Our bases burned. Our resources were plundered. We were ashed.

"He used us as a weapon, then discarded us like spent ammunition."
— Alliance Records

To this day, powerstrokeking123 remains our sworn enemy. This is not just strategy. This is personal.

We will find him. We will ash him. The debt will be paid. 🎯

The WOUT Adoption Program

Redemption From enemies to brothers
February 2026

After the betrayal, something unexpected happened. Members of WOUT began to leave their alliance — disillusioned, frustrated, looking for a new home.

Where did they come? To us.

We made a sacred vow: we would adopt every single player with "GUY" in their name who left WOUT. It became a running joke, then a tradition, then a point of honor.

"If your name has GUY in it and you're leaving WOUT? Welcome home, brother."
— captpoptart

Today, the GUYs of HELIX stand as living proof that redemption is possible. They came as refugees. They stayed as family. 💪

If you're a GUY still stuck in WOUT... we're waiting for you. 🚪

The Iron Reign of captpoptart

Legend Leadership between lectures
Ongoing

They say great leaders are forged in fire. captpoptart was forged between chemistry class and organic lab.

By day, he balances equations and memorizes molecular structures. By night (and during boring lectures), he commands HELIX with an iron fist wrapped in a velvet glove.

"I have a midterm tomorrow, but these bases aren't going to ash themselves."
— captpoptart, probably

His leadership style:

• Strategic brilliance disguised as "just vibes"
• Zero tolerance for NAP breakers
• Motivational speeches delivered via Discord at 2am
• Somehow always online when you need him

Under his reign, HELIX has grown from a scrappy group of survivors to a force that makes Top 10 alliances nervous. All while maintaining a GPA that would make his parents proud (allegedly).

Long live the captain. Long live the tart. 👑
