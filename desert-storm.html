What to Expect: Your First Desert Storm

1 No Real Losses!

Everything that happens here is a simulation.

Any troops "killed" go to a special event hospital. They do not die, and healing them is free (it only costs time/speed-ups).

You get all your troops back perfectly fine as soon as the match ends.

2 The 3-Squad Rule

You will be busy!

Squad 1: Use this to attack or defend main buildings.

Squad 2 & 3: Use these to capture "Oil Refineries" and "Supply Boxes" across the map.

Pro Tip: Keep your squads moving! Standing still is how you get targeted.

3 Teleporting is Key

You have limited teleports. Only jump when necessary.

Priority: If a "Whale" (a massive enemy player) teleports next to you, LEAVE.

Don't try to defend your base; you'll just give them free points. Teleport to the other side of the map and keep capturing small buildings.

4 Watch for Supply Boxes

Every few minutes, boxes appear on the map. They give massive points.

If you see a box near you, grab it immediately!

Even if you lose every fight, you can win the game just by collecting boxes.
