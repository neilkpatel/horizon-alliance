Honorable Campaign Tactics

Pushing vs. Farming
Strategy Events

Team HLIX, getting stuck on a level in the Honorable Campaign happens to everyone. Here is how you keep your progression moving when you hit a wall:

  • 📱 The "Gear Strip" Strategy: If your 1st Squad is stuck on a tough level, strip the best UR gear off your 2nd and 3rd squads and put it on your 1st Squad's weaker links.
  • 🚗 Farming vs. Pushing: If you absolutely cannot beat a new level even after swapping gear, stop wasting attempts. Fall back and farm the highest level you can comfortably "Quick Fight" to 100% completion. This maximizes your hourly rewards until your squads are strong enough to push again.

Gear Leveling & Progression Rules

Crucial Gear & Squad Priority (The 10-20-30-40 Rule)
Gear Strategy

Team HLIX, managing your gear is the difference between winning and losing fights. Here are the hard rules for gear progression:

  • 🏅 100% Focus on Squad 1: Your 1st Squad wins wars. Do not spread your best gear out among your backup squads. Feed your best items to your main hitters first.
  • ♻️ Dismantle Blue & Purple: Once you start getting Legendary (UR) gear, immediately dismantle your old Blue and Purple gear. They are only useful for the ore they provide to upgrade your UR pieces.
  • 🎯 The 10-20-30-40 Rule: When leveling up your UR gear, keep it balanced. Level all pieces to 10, then all to 20, then 30, and so on. Do not max one piece to 40 while the rest sit at 0. Balanced gear gives your squad the survivability they need to win.

Mid-Day Gear & Boss Hacks

Mid-Day Tips: UR Gear & Boss Damage
Gear Events

Team HLIX, here are two quick tips to keep your progression on track and your damage high:

🎨 The UR Gear Trap

Once your 1st Squad has a full set of UR gear, do not start blindly crafting UR gear for your 2nd Squad. I made this mistake myself, and I want to make sure my mistake doesn't turn into your mistake too.

  • The Cost: Adding stars is a massive power spike but incredibly expensive. It costs 1,110 Dielectric Ceramic per stage (5 stages total) plus 50 Blueprints just to reach 1 star.
  • The Exception: If you absolutely must craft UR gear for your 2nd squad right now, only craft Railguns and Data Chips for your main attackers, then stop!
  • The Fix: Funnel the rest of your Ceramics and Blueprints into starring up your 1st Squad until they are rolling with star-powered gear. They are the ones who win wars!

🎯 Wanted Boss Damage Hack

The daily Code Boss is a 30-second DPS check and doesn't hit hard enough to wipe you out. To maximize your 3 daily hits, go pure offense.

  • The Swap: Check the daily "Code" (50% boost to Tanks, Aircraft, or Missiles). Drop a heavy defender from your front line and swap in your highest-damage attacker from that boosted troop type. This swap could be the extra juice you need to hit the next damage goal and reap the massive rewards!
