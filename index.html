★ Welcome to Horizon

To grow fast and help the alliance win, you need to master the "Daily Rhythm."

In this game, when you click a button is more important than what you click.

Where to Find These on Your Screen

Alliance VS (The Duel)

Tap the "VS" icon on the right side of your screen.

This is a week-long battle against another alliance.

We need everyone to hit 9 chests daily!

Arms Race

Tap the "Events" icon (top right) → Arms Race.

These are 4-hour mini-challenges that reset throughout the day.

📅 How to Find the Arms Race Schedule

The Arms Race schedule is fairly predictable but it can change, so here's how you can check what is coming up in the next segment.

You don't have to guess what's coming next! Here is how to see the "Clock":

Open Events

Tap the "Events" icon (top right of your main screen) and select the "Arms Race" tab.

Check the Current Phase

You will see the current mission (e.g., "Base Building") and a countdown timer showing how much time is left in this 4-hour window.

See the Full Day

Tap the Calendar/List icon (usually in the top right corner of the Arms Race screen). This opens the Stage Preview.

Plan Ahead

This list shows you all 6 phases for today and exactly what time they start. Use this to plan when you'll finish your buildings or start your research!

🏆 The 4 Golden Rules for New Members

1

The "Double Dip" Strategy

Never use speedups just because you have them.

Wait for the Arms Race theme to match the VS Day.

Example:

On Wednesday (Research Day), wait until the Arms Race says "Tech Research." When you finish a tech now, you get rewards from both places at once.

2

Save Your "Red Dots"

You will see red dots on your Radar missions.

Complete the mission but do NOT click the reward.

Save them for Monday, Wednesday, or Friday.

Clicking them on the right day gives our alliance millions of points for free!
3

The 9-Chest Goal

Your goal is to reach the 9th chest in the VS menu every day.

The rewards in that 9th chest (Valor Badges) are the only way to unlock high-level troops.

If you spend your resources on the wrong day, you will never reach it.

You may only get the first six at first. Don't worry, we'll make sure you get all nine ASAP! A separate guide on research optimization is coming soon.
4

Stop Spending That Hero XP!!

Yes, we know, we all want a level 100 Kimberly.

But please remember this is a game of long-term strategy, and it is only as a team that we will develop the squads.

Don't worry, we all started by spending everything.

⚠ NAP — Non-Aggression Pact

EVERYONE. LISTEN. UP.

NAP is a peace treaty between the top alliances on our server.

We at Horizon have worked incredibly hard to get into the top ten alliances, and we want to stay here. Please read the below and make sure you understand. If you don't understand, please ask!

Why Do We Have It?

If we spend all week burning each other's bases, we waste our resources, lose our troops, and become too weak to fight the real enemy: other servers.

NAP allows us to grow in peace so we can dominate together during the weekend Warzone Duels.

The NAP Rules (Usually NAP 10)

Most servers use NAP 10, which means the Top 10 Alliances in the power rankings are forbidden from attacking each other.

NO Base Hits

Do not attack the headquarters of any player in a NAP alliance.

NO Resource Tile Hits

If someone from a NAP alliance is gathering at a gold or iron mine, leave them alone.

NO Truck Plundering

During the Intercity Truck event, only plunder trucks from alliances outside the top 10 (or players with no alliance tag).

What Happens If You Break NAP?

If you accidentally attack a NAP player, it is called a "NAP Violation."

  • The other alliance will send a screenshot of the attack to our leaders (R4/R5).
  • You will usually be asked to pay back the resources you stole.
  • Repeated violations can get you kicked from the alliance to protect the rest of the team from a "Retaliation Strike."

💡 Pro Tip

Always check a player's Alliance Tag (the 3 letters next to their name) before you hit them. If those letters are in the Top 10 rankings, DO NOT ATTACK.
