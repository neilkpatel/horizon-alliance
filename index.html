Where to Find VS & Arms Race

Alliance VS (The Duel)

Tap the "VS" icon on the right side of your screen. This is a week-long battle against another alliance.

We need everyone to hit 9 chests daily!

Arms Race

Tap the "Events" icon (top right) → Arms Race. These are 4-hour mini-challenges that reset throughout the day.

How to Find the Arms Race Schedule

Open Events

Tap "Events" (top right) → "Arms Race".

Check Current Phase

See the current mission and countdown timer for this 4-hour window.

See Full Day

Tap the Calendar/List icon to open the Stage Preview with all 6 phases.

The 4 Golden Rules

1 Double Dip: Wait for Arms Race to match VS Day before using speedups
2 Red Dots: Complete radar missions, but save rewards for Mon/Wed/Fri
3 9 Chests: Hit the 9th VS chest daily for Valor Badges
4 Hero XP: Don't spend it yet — long-term strategy wins
Full rule details + examples
1

The "Double Dip" Strategy

Never use speedups just because you have them. Wait for the Arms Race theme to match the VS Day.

Example:

On Wednesday (Research Day), wait until Arms Race says "Tech Research." You get rewards from both places at once.

Day 5 (Training Day) is HUGE: Training speedups give 4x the points compared to other days. Start long upgrades BEFORE the event phase, then finish them DURING the scoring window.
2

Save Your "Red Dots"

Complete Radar missions but do NOT click the reward. Save them for Monday, Wednesday, or Friday.

Clicking on the right day gives our alliance millions of points for free!
3

The 9-Chest Goal

Reach the 9th chest in VS every day. Valor Badges are the only way to unlock high-level troops.

Starting out, you may only get 6 chests. That's okay — we'll help you reach 9 ASAP!
4

Stop Spending Hero XP!!

Yes, we all want a level 100 Kimberly. But this is a long-term strategy game. We develop squads as a team.

Don't worry, we all started by spending everything.
NAP — Non-Aggression Pact

EVERYONE. LISTEN. UP.

NAP is a peace treaty between the top alliances on our server.

We've worked incredibly hard to get into the top ten. If confused, ask!

Why Do We Have It?

If we spend all week burning each other, we become too weak to fight other servers. NAP lets us grow in peace so we can dominate together during Warzone Duels.

The NAP Rules (NAP 10)

Top 10 Alliances are forbidden from attacking each other:

NO Base Hits

Do not attack any player's headquarters in a NAP alliance.

NO Resource Tile Hits

If someone from a NAP alliance is gathering, leave them alone.

NO Truck Plundering

Only plunder trucks from alliances outside the top 10.

What Happens If You Break NAP?

  • The other alliance sends a screenshot to our R4/R5.
  • You'll be asked to pay back stolen resources.
  • Repeated violations = kicked to protect the team.

Pro Tip

Always check the Alliance Tag (3 letters next to their name) before attacking. If they're Top 10, DO NOT ATTACK.

FAQ

🦸 Which heroes should I focus on?

S-Tier (Build these first):

• Kimberly (Tank/Attack) — The one hero to rule them all. Get her in your first offer.

• DVA (Aircraft/Attack) — Strongest damage dealer in the game.

• Murphy (Tank/Defense) — Amazing defensive buffs for longer fights.

• Tesla (Aircraft/Attack) — Massive energy damage, great against armor.

Avoid: Kane, Ambolt, Swift, McGregor — they fall off hard in late game.

Rule: Focus on 2-3 S-tier heroes. Don't spread upgrades across too many!

🏗️ What should I prioritize upgrading?

1. Headquarters — Determines troop tiers, building limits, unlocks features. Upgrade ASAP.

2. Tech Center — Unlocks at HQ level 7. Get a 2nd at District 102, 3rd at HQ 30.

3. Resource Buildings — Keep upgraded to fund everything else.

4. Barracks — More troops = more power.

• Secret tasks (Yours/Assist/Loot)

• 3 Campaign battlefields

• 1 hero + 1 survivor recruitment

• Armored truck missions

• Get on the train

📡 How do I use the Radar effectively?

Check the Radar frequently — you'll find battles, item drops, and new survivors.

Don't miss survivors! They contribute major skills in gardening, building, and production.

Key tip: Complete Radar tasks daily, but save the REWARDS for Mon/Wed/Fri (Radar & Drone days in VS).

🔄 What's the survivor swap trick?

You can switch survivors between buildings to apply their bonuses multiple times!

Example: Keep your best survivors in Tech Center #1 to start a research, then move them to Tech Center #2 before starting another. Both get the bonus!

⚔️ Army strategy: Quantity or quality?

Early game: Focus on QUANTITY. Get as many troops as possible first.

Mid/Late game: Start upgrading troop quality once you have decent numbers.

Key insight: Build ONE strong march first, not multiple weak ones.
