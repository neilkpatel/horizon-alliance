Where to Find VS & Arms Race

Alliance VS (The Duel)

Tap the "VS" icon on the right side of your screen.

This is a week-long battle against another alliance.

We need everyone to hit 9 chests daily!

Arms Race

These are 4-hour mini-challenges that reset throughout the day.

How to Find the Arms Race Schedule

The Arms Race schedule is fairly predictable but it can change. Here's how to check what's coming next:

Open Events

Tap the "Events" icon (top right) and select "Arms Race".

Check Current Phase

You'll see the current mission and countdown timer for this 4-hour window.

See Full Day

Tap the Calendar/List icon to open the Stage Preview.

Plan Ahead

This shows all 6 phases and their start times. Plan your builds and research!

The 4 Golden Rules (Full Details)
1

The "Double Dip" Strategy

Never use speedups just because you have them.

Wait for the Arms Race theme to match the VS Day.

Example:

On Wednesday (Research Day), wait until Arms Race says "Tech Research." You get rewards from both places at once.

2

Save Your "Red Dots"

Complete Radar missions but do NOT click the reward.

Save them for Monday, Wednesday, or Friday.

Clicking on the right day gives our alliance millions of points for free!
3

The 9-Chest Goal

Reach the 9th chest in VS every day.

Valor Badges are the only way to unlock high-level troops.

Starting out, you may only get 6 chests. That's okay ‚Äî we'll help you reach 9 ASAP!
4

Stop Spending Hero XP!!

Yes, we all want a level 100 Kimberly.

But this is a long-term strategy game. We develop squads as a team.

Don't worry, we all started by spending everything.

EVERYONE. LISTEN. UP.

If we spend all week burning each other, we become too weak to fight other servers. NAP lets us grow in peace so we can dominate together during Warzone Duels.

Top 10 Alliances are forbidden from attacking each other:

NO Base Hits

NO Resource Tile Hits

If someone from a NAP alliance is gathering, leave them alone.

NO Truck Plundering

Only plunder trucks from alliances outside the top 10.

  • The other alliance sends a screenshot to our R4/R5.
  • You'll be asked to pay back stolen resources.
